Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Molten PE is pushed through a die with tightly controlled temperature zones for consistent melt pressure and homogeneous pipe structure.

  • Barrier screw design for melt quality
  • Stable throughput monitoring

Controlled Cooling

Calibrated water baths remove heat gradually to lock in dimensions and reduce residual stress before sizing.

  • Multi-stage temperature profiling
  • Closed-loop circulation

Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum calibration keeps the pipe round while air pressure stabilises internal geometry for tight OD/ID tolerances.

  • Precision vacuum sizing tanks
  • Wall thickness uniformity checks

Quality Control

In-line gauges and batch sampling validate mechanical properties, surface finish, and marking legibility before release.

  • Dimensional verification
  • Traceability batch records

Marking & Traceability

Laser or inkjet marking applies standards-compliant print for diameter, class, lot, and manufacturer identification.

  • ISO-aligned legend layout
  • Contrast-tested print

Cutting to Length

Servo pullers and planetary cutters deliver square ends and repeatable coil lengths for yard dispatch or drum packing.

  • Burr-free cut quality
  • Length tolerance control

Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, strapped, and labelled for safe transport with handling instructions aligned to site logistics.

  • UV-protected stretch wrap
  • Coil weight optimisation
